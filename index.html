What is AI Guardrailing?

AI guardrailing is the practice of implementing safety boundaries, constraints, and control mechanisms that ensure AI systems operate within defined ethical, legal, and operational parameters. Just as physical guardrails protect drivers from dangerous edges, AI guardrails protect organizations from AI-generated risks including harmful content, biased outputs, data leakage, and unauthorized actions.

At RazorSecurity, we believe that guardrailing is not about limiting AI's potential‚Äîit's about channeling its power responsibly. Our guardrailing approach combines technical controls, behavioral monitoring, and adaptive constraints that evolve with your AI systems while maintaining strict security boundaries.
